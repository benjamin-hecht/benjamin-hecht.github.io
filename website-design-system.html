Text Styles

h1: Main Heading

  • Font-size: 2.5rem
  • Font-family: Arial
  • Background-color: Dark Green (on the website it's an image)
  • Color: White

h2: Subheading

  • Font-size: 1.5rem
  • Font-family: Arial
  • Background-color: Dark Green (on the website it's an image)
  • Color: White

p: Paragraph

  • Font-size: 1rem
  • Font-family: Arial
  • Background-color: Beige
  • Color: Black

HEADER: Header Links

  • Font-size: 1rem
  • Font-family: Arial
  • Text-transform: Uppercase
  • Background-color: Dark Green
  • Color: White

footer: Footer Links

  • Font-size: 1rem
  • Font-family: Arial
  • Background-color: Dark Green
  • Color: White
